About

Dr. Kannan is a metabolomist, clinical researcher, author, healthcare project developer specializing in diabetes management and preventing heart diseases arising from diabetes. His holistic approach focuses on understanding the pathogenic process involved in the development of type 2 diabetes, enabling personalized and effective management strategies.
